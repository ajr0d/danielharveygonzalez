About Bellwether Town Notopia!

Notopia!

The notion of heterotopia was defined by French philosopher Michel Foucault as a space that is somehow ‘other’, an unusual milieu suspended between the real and the unreal. Neither dystopian nor utopian; the heterotopia is just strange.

Throughout the Covid-19 pandemic, the United Kingdom has born the hallmarks of speculative disaster fiction: panicked stockpiling of foodstuffs, police constabularies granted exceptional powers, random police checkpoints, and the deification of state apparatus. The list goes on. Rather than with a bang, these things happened without theatricality and with the population’s encouragement.

“Notopia!” (2020) is a visual response to that idea of heterotopia within the context of the UK government’s reaction to Covid-19, highlighting evidence of the paranoid Coronavirus landscape and exploring that unnerving sensation of existing within a space that is ‘other’.

With thanks to the GANE Trust for their support in realising this body of work.
